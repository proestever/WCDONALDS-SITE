WcDonald's is a unique NFT collection featuring 15 anime girls inspired by a McDonald's ad campaign, including the rare "Celestia, the Spectral Fast Food Princess." This collection stands out for its diversity and the rarity of certain NFTs like Celestia. It's underpinned by a total of 10,000 PRC-X tokens, with dynamic minting and burning of NFTs to keep the ecosystem engaging. The collection uses the ERC-X token standard, an experimental blend of multiple ERC standards for optimized gas efficiency, scalability, and features like adjustable tokens per NFT and anti-snipe mechanisms.
